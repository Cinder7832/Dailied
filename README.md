# Dailied

**Dailied** is a free mobile app for Android that gives you and your friends the same three random real-life tasks every day. Complete your dailies, build a streak, earn points, help friends with gifts, and rank up.

**Website:** (https://cinder7832.github.io/Dailied/)

---

## Features

### Daily challenges

- **Three shared dailies** — Everyone receives the same three randomly selected tasks each UK calendar day
- **Real-life activities** — Simple, playful challenges designed to add something different to your day
- **One-tap completion** — Mark a task as complete and see your score and streak update immediately
- **Undo completions** — Tap a completed task again before the day ends to reverse it
- **Daily reset** — New challenges arrive each day based on Europe/London time
- **Daily reminders** — Choose an optional morning, noon, or evening notification on your device

### Score, streaks & saves

- Earn the points shown on each task you complete
- Build a streak by completing at least one daily each day
- Receive a streak bonus of 1% per previous streak day, up to a 25% bonus
- Earn a **streak save** after every seven real completion days in an unbroken streak
- Use streak saves to protect missed days and keep your visible streak and score multiplier
- Choose whether to use saves or reset your streak when recovery is needed

### History & ranks

- **Task history** — Browse every daily you have completed, grouped by day
- Search your completed tasks and sort them from newest or oldest
- See missed days that were protected by streak saves
- **Leaderboards** — Compare daily, weekly, and all-time scores
- View player profile pictures, streaks, scores, names, usernames, and bios from the ranks
- Gifts never affect earned score or leaderboard position, keeping rankings fair

### Comments & community

- Open a shared comment thread for any daily task
- Reply to other players in one-level conversations
- Like or dislike comments
- Edit or delete your own comments
- See player names, usernames, profile pictures, timestamps, and edited labels

### Gifts

- Search for other Dailied players
- Send settled points or streak saves to a friend
- Accept or reject incoming gift requests
- Cancel a pending gift before the receiver responds
- Pending point gifts reserve their amount and expire after seven UK days if unanswered
- Search and filter your incoming and outgoing gift history

### Account & Breeze connection

- Sign in with email and password or Google
- Use the same account and shared profile as **Breeze Client**
- Create a new account with a display name or reuse an existing Breeze account
- Edit your shared display name, profile picture, and bio from Dailied
- Change your password, request a password-reset email, or sign out
- Follow a first-time guide explaining dailies, scoring, streaks, gifts, saves, history, and ranks

### Personalization

- Choose between **system**, **light**, and **dark** themes
- Enjoy a calm, warm, editorial design with subtle animations
- Reopen the **How Dailied works** guide at any time from your profile

### Admin tools

- Admin-only task management hidden from regular users
- Create reusable tasks with descriptions and custom point values
- Search, edit, enable, disable, or remove unused tasks from the task library
- Dailied automatically selects three active tasks for everyone each day
- Reset a player's Dailied score, streak, and history when required
